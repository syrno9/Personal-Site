About hikari3.ch


hikari3.ch is an imageboard I founded in May 2023, and I still reign as the current administrator.

    "A very comfy imageboard for people that like to take it easy. To get started, read the rules, then read the FAQ. After that, you can start posting!"

I didn't really make the site for much reason. I was just kind of curious what it would be like to host my own imageboard and thought it would be fun.

Brief History: I had been browsing Heyuri for a bit since mid 2022. Rule 8, and the site's culture was one of the main inspirations behind hikari3. The "comfy atmosphere" was inspired by wapchan. I started working on it late April 2023, and put together a crappily done front-end made from Aleph (8chan.moe's front-end). I asked my friend (MEMEow) for some art of our mascot (Hikari-chan) and then asked my friend to make an email for kaguya (Heyuri's admin) asking if I could link the site. (I wasn't sure how to make a good email for this).

Turns out I was supposed to ask on their /q/ board but either-way, it'd probably be better to just post about it on /b/ instead since linking new sites isn't common.

I posted about it on May 20/21 2023 and that was one of the only times I advertised it myself
